User Entity

Attributes:

user_id (PK)

username

password

role (Patient/Doctor)

Relationships:

One-to-One relationship with Patient (if the user is a Patient).

One-to-One relationship with Doctor (if the user is a Doctor).

Patient Entity

Attributes:

patient_id (PK)

first_name

last_name

date_of_birth

gender

phone_number

email

blood_group

address

registration_date

Relationships:

One-to-One relationship with User (for authentication purposes).

One-to-Many relationship with Appointment (A Patient can have multiple Appointments).

One-to-Many relationship with Consultation (A Patient can have multiple Consultations).

Doctor Entity

Attributes:

doctor_id (PK)

first_name

last_name

specialization

contact_number

email

license_number

availability_schedule

Relationships:

One-to-One relationship with User (for authentication purposes).

One-to-Many relationship with Appointment (A Doctor can have multiple Appointments).

One-to-Many relationship with Consultation (A Doctor can have multiple Consultations).

Appointment Entity

Attributes:

appointment_id (PK)

appointment_date

time_slot

reason_for_visit

status (Scheduled, Completed, Cancelled)

doctor_id (FK)

patient_id (FK)

Relationships:

Many-to-One relationship with Patient (Each Appointment is booked by one Patient).

Many-to-One relationship with Doctor (Each Appointment is with one Doctor).

One-to-One relationship with Consultation (Each Appointment results in one Consultation).

Consultation Entity

Attributes:

consultation_id (PK)

consultation_date

reason_for_visit

notes (Doctor's notes)

appointment_id (FK)

prescription_id (FK, Optional)

bill_id (FK, Optional)

Relationships:

One-to-One relationship with Appointment (Each Appointment results in one Consultation).

Many-to-One relationship with Patient (A Patient can have multiple Consultations).

Many-to-One relationship with Doctor (A Doctor can create multiple Consultations).

One-to-One relationship with Bill (Each Consultation generates one Bill).

One-to-One relationship with Prescription (Each Consultation may have one Prescription).

Bill Entity

Attributes:

bill_id (PK)

consultation_id (FK)

total_amount

payment_status (Paid, Pending)

due_date

issue_date

Relationships:

One-to-One relationship with Consultation (Each Bill is linked to a Consultation).

One-to-One relationship with Payment (Each Bill has one Payment).

Payment Entity

Attributes:

payment_id (PK)

bill_id (FK)

amount_paid

payment_method (Credit Card, Online Transfer, etc.)

payment_date

Relationships:

One-to-One relationship with Bill (Each Payment is linked to one Bill).

Prescription Entity

Attributes:

prescription_id (PK)

consultation_id (FK)

medication_name

dosage_mg

price

frequency

start_date

end_date

Relationships:

One-to-One relationship with Consultation (Each Consultation can have one Prescription).

Many-to-One relationship with InventoryItem (A Prescription may reference multiple medications from the inventory).

InventoryItem Entity

Attributes:

inventory_item_id (PK)

item_name

category

unit_price

quantity

supplier_info

description

Relationships:

Many-to-One relationship with Prescription (A Medication from the Inventory can be prescribed in many Prescriptions).
